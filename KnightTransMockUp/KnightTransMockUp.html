Welcome to the Knight Transportation home page!

Here at Knight we strive to take the best care of our customers and drivers. We constantly do research about logisitcs, fuel efficency, the current demand in the market, our environmental impact, and cutting edge technology in the trucking industry. We care about ensuring our customers get the best prices available through these efforts. We look forward to working with our customers, and we would love to hear any feedback our consumers may have. Please click here to contact us with your feedback


Our drivers are one of our number one priorities. We strive to select the safest and most experienced professional drivers to work with us. We strive to make our drivers comfortable, and to give our drivers better pay packages than our competitors. We offer health insurance, disability, and life insurance at reasonable prices. We also offer many different packages for milage pay and even hwo often our drivers would like to be paid. Best of all we have various bonuses that can be obtained every quarter, and in most cases our drivers can get home as often as every week.
